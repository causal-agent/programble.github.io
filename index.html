Hi, I'm Curtis. I am a 19-year-old software engineer from Montreal, Quebec.

I am a web developer at Busbud, an online bus travel startup.

Outside of work, I also enjoy coding hobby projects and contributing to open-source software. My projects and contributions can be found on GitHub. I work on Ubuntu and mostly write JavaScript, Ruby or C.

Other than software, I'm interested in music and collecting records. My collection mostly consists of local post-rock, including Godspeed You! Black Emperor and Thee Silver Mt. Zion Memorial Orchestra. Some of my other favourite artists are mewithoutYou, Arcade Fire and Neutral Milk Hotel.
