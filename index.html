Hi, I'm Curtis. I am a 19-year-old software developer from Montréal, Québec.

I work at Busbud, an online bus travel startup located in Mile End.

Outside of work, I also enjoy working on hobby projects and contributing to open-source software. All of my public work can be found on GitHub. These days I'm mostly working with Node.js or C on OS X.

My main interest after software development is music. I collect records, mainly consisting of post-rock, such as Godspeed You! Black Emperor and Thee Silver Mt. Zion Memorial Orchestra. Other artists in my collection include Swans, mewithoutYou and Neutral Milk Hotel.
